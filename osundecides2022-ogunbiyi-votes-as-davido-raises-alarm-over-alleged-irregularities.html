Ogunbiyi Votes As Davido Raises Alarm Over Alleged Irregularities

Publish date: 0001-01-01

The governorship candidate for Accord Party in the Osun State gubernatorial election, Akin Ogunbiyi has cast his vote.

Naija News understands that Ogunbiyi was at his polling unit; PU03, Ward 05 located at Methodist Primary School, Ileogbo, Ayedire Local Government Area of the State this morning.

The governorship hopeful exercised his civic rights as he hope to defeat his counterpart from the ruling All Progressives Congress, Governor Adegboyega Otetola and candidates from other opposition parties.

Meanwhile, Nigerian musician, David Adedeji Adeleke, popularly known as Davido, has raised an alarm over an alleged attack on electorates suspected to be supporters of the PDP.

A netizen [@SodiqTade] had claimed in a Twitter post that people are not allowed to vote for PDP in a named polling unit.

The Twitter user wrote: “People are not allowed to vote for PDP at Ok 3, Unit 14. They are beating People perceived to PDP supporters there…this is happening at ogbon oya in Ile life”.

Reacting, Davido quoted the tweet adding the official handle of the Independent National Electoral Commission [@inecnigeria] and that of the Nigeria Police Force [@NigeriapolicePR] with a caption ‘Alert’.

In another post, Davido wrote: “They r not allowing our supporters vote in IFE. @inecnigeria”
