Upcoming Artiste Boldly Carries Banner Looking for Rich, Older Woman

Publish date: 2024-07-11
  • Magbee 254 Msanii, an upcoming artist, took to the streets looking for an older woman to finance his lifestyle
  • Magbee was spotted with a banner near Kenya National Archives detailing the sort of sponsor he wants
  • The young man said times are tough, and he needs an extra source of income to finance his lifestyle

An upcoming Kenyan musician stunned Kenyans after boldly walking with a banner, looking for a sponsor.

The musician known as Mgbee 254 Msanii was spotted at National Archives, Nairobi, with the banner announcing his intentions.

According to Magbee, he is looking for a rich, older woman to finance his lifestyle. Some of the requirements he listed for the sponsor included having a big behind and driving an expensive car.

"I am looking for a sponsor. Big woman with a big behind driving a big car with a V8 engine," his poster read.

Tough economic times

His banner attracted the attention of passers-by, and one of them inquired why he had opted for the bold move.

Magbee decried the state of the economy, saying the money he makes is not enough. According to the musician, pursuing music is expensive, and he needs an extra income source.

“I am broke. The economy is bad. I need to pay rent, electricity and Wi-Fi. I have a job, but the money is not enough.“I also need to do a video shoot, and one video costs KSh 25,000,” he said.
