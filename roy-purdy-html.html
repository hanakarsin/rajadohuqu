Roy Purdy - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-28

Comedian and rapper notable for his viral "RunningManChallenge" and "when u graduated af" videos, among others. He has more than 3 million YouTube subscribers and 2.2 million followers on Instagram. His music videos have gained him millions of views on his self-titled YouTube account. 

Before Fame

He began recording original songs his freshman year of high school, and began taking music seriously as a junior.

Trivia

He grew up a skateboarder as well. One of his first music video releases was called "Skate Rhyme." His Instagram has featured multiple videos of him skateboarding. 

Family Life

His little brother George has filmed a large number of his videos.

Associated With

One of his top videos became a parody dance video set to Zay Hilfigerrr and Zayion McCall's "Juju On That Beat."
