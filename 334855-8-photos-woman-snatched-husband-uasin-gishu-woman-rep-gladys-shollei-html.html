Publish date: 2024-07-21

- Faith Rono joined Standard Media Group as an intern and later a correspondent

- Ex-company's CEO Sam Shollei promoted her to head the corporate affairs department after the two became lovers

The marriage of ex-Standard Media Group boss Sam Shollei and his politician wife Gladys Boss Shollei is dead with the former having found another lover.

Shollei officiated his relationship with former junior staff turned lover, Faith Rono, when he and family and friends made a long journey to Chepkorio village to seek elders' blessings.

The engagement ceremony known as koito went down at Rono's paternal home in Elgeyo Marakwet county on Friday, January 10.

The elders approved of the union and the groom's request for Rono's hand in marriage.

Though the dowry price remained a heavily-guarded secret among those who attended, it is clear two are now at liberty to choose whether to move in officially or conduct a wedding.

It was not too long before cheating allegations rocked Shollei's 26-year-old marriage.

The palatial multi-million home and another one in Uasin Gishu are now at the centre of the messy divorce, with Shollei and Gladys each demanding equal share.
