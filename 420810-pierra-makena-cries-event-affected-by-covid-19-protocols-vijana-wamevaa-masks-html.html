Renowned female DJ Pierra Makena is counting losses after her Park and Chill event, in Meru, was cancelled at the last minute due to the government's COVID-19 protocols.

The entertainer has been holding outdoor events where revellers show up with their cars and relax while enjoying some great music mixes from her.

Her latest event was to take place on Saturday, July 31, in Meru but ended up not happening after her team was ordered to shut down everything they had begun.

In a video shared by @bennytothedj on Twitter, Makena could be seen ranting at the press, bemoaning having to lose so much money and stopping young men from doing honest work.

"How are we going to survive? I'm as disappointed as it can get. Watching these young people having put an effort since 4am, there is nothing wrong they have done, they could have chosen other things... but this is the work they chose to do, they are masked up. We agreed to obey all this rules but we are now told we can not do it," she ranted.

Makena said there was nothing she could do as she had already suffered losses but only to return home.

The entertainer also went on her Instagram page sharing a photo from the cancelled event while noting how frustrated she was by the country.

She said:

"This is 12pm today. Meru and my fans were ready. Even with all the regulations given they were ready to obey but make sure my homecoming works. We got all approvals, we did everything right, why would my own governor @hon_kiraitu_murungi say we be stopped," she wrote.

She claimed no rule was broken, and people were chilling in their cars and accused the Meru county government of treating them like they had robbed a bank.

Makena's daughter's birthday

In some exciting Makena news, about two weeks ago, Makena's daughter Ricca got the ultimate birthday party as she turned five years old on Monday, July 19.

On the following day, the mother of one surprised her princess with a unicorn-themed birthday party at the Carnivore grounds.

In videos shared by the DJ on her Instagram page, Ricca and her age mates arrived at the venue in a stunning white limousine with cute decorations.

They stepped out donning lovely colourful dresses, with Ricca being the first to alight. They were received by Pierra and another greeter and given directions.

The entertainer also shared a lovely video of the moment Ricca saw her birthday decorations and the beautiful cake, all made with the unicorn theme in mind.

Emotions overcame Ricca as she marvelled at the cute setting and asked her mother if she did all that for her.

"Mum you did all this for me?," She asked.

Pierra confirmed that it was all for her and said the moment made her teary - in her caption to the post.
