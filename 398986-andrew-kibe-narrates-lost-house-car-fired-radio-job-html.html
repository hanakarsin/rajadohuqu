Andrew Kibe narrates how he lost his house, car after being fired from radio job

Publish date: 2024-04-09

- Kibe disclosed he lost his car and also became homeless for a while after ditching Kiss 100

- He, however, bounced back in late 2020 and launched his own online radio dubbed Rogue Radio

- His testimony inspired and encouraged many Kenyans who lauded him for his resiliance

If there is a year that many people across the world would want deleted from their lives' timelines, it must be the year 2020.

Many people were affected differently when it became clear coronavirus had become a worldwide pandemic.

Jobs were lost so were other means of livelihood for many people and Rogue Radio CEO Andrew Kibe, was not an exception.

The former NRG Radio and Kiss 100 presenter recently took to social media to inspire and encourage young people about life with his own life testimony.

Kibe, in an Instagram video seen by TUKO.co.ke disclosed that 2020 hit him so bad, especially after losing his job at Kiss 100.

According to the radio host, he thought he was headed for the stars when misfortunes came knocking.

Kibe said he lost his car and was rendered homeless for a while after losing his job at Kiss 100.

He, however, inspired many after revealing it was in the same 2020 when things also started to ease up for him.

The loud radio host said he was able to launch his own internet radio dubbed Rogue Radio after recollecting himself and giving life yet another shot.

"I was homeless. I thought I would make it the next day after quitting my job, but no. That journey is long. 2020 messed up with me big time but rewarded me at the same time. I am proud of my Rogue Radio project. With this radio, I am going somewhere." Kibe said.

His story touched many of his followers and other Kenyans who camped on the comments section of his post to thank him for encouraging them.

TUKO.co.ke reported how Kibe ditched Kiss 100 when the station brought on board comedian Jalang'o who had been axed from Milele FM.

Upon landing at the Lions Place based radio station, he replaced Kibe who was by then co-hosting alongside BFF Kamene Goro.

Today, he runs Rogue Radio where he is also a presenter doing the drive show.
