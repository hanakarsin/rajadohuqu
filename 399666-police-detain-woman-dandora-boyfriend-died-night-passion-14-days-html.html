Dandora woman whose boyfriend died during night of passion detained for 14 more days

Publish date: 2024-02-29

- Joyce Wairimu will be held for a further 14 days in connection to the death of her boyfriend Robert Maina

- Maina died on the night of Wednesday, January 6, after a night of lovemaking with her

- Investigators are reportedly investigating the woman for murder

A Makadara court has ordered the detention of a woman whose Dandora boyfriend died during sexual intercourse on the night of Wednesday, January 6.

The late Kenyatta National Hospital nurse met his death while having sex with Joyce Wairimu at his rental house in Dandora Estate.

According to Nairobi News, sleuths from the Directorate of Criminal Investigations (DCI) received the go-ahead to hold Wairimu for a further 14 days pending the completion of investigations.

The investigators are reportedly investigating the woman for murder and she will in the meantime be held at the Dandora Police Station.

Comprehensive investigations

Detective Josephat Wafula said there was a need to conduct a comprehensive probe, get the medical reports of the suspect and also conduct an autopsy of the deceased.

"There are some assorted drugs recovered from the deceased’s house and remains of food that is yet to be taken to the government chemist for analysis for further action," he said.

As reported by TUKO.co.ke earlier, Maina was having a juicy moment with his lover Wairimu when he got stuck midway and breathed his last.

A police report from Dandora Police Station indicated Wairimu was perturbed by the incident and rushed to the station to make a report.

Police officers visited the scene and found Maina lying lifeless on his bed.

"He fainted and died instantly while on the act. At his house, assorted drugs and medical report summary were found," read the police report in part.
