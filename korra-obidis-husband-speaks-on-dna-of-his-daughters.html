Korra Obidi's Husband Speaks On DNA Of His Daughters

Publish date: 2024-05-27

Justin Dean, the husband of Nigerian dancer Korra Obidi, has spoken on the DNA of his daughters.

Recall that Naija News earlier reported that Justin announced that he and Korra were getting a divorce. He hinted on her being a cheat as the reason for their separation.

This outburst made some people suggest he carries out a paternity test on the girls to make sure they are biologically his.

However, the father of two has taken to his Instagram account to speak on his daughters paternity, stating in no uncertain terms that they are biologically his.

He uploaded a picture of himself together with his first daughter and his new born with the caption: “My flesh, my blood and my heart.”
