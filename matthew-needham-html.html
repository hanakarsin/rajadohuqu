Matthew Needham - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-04

British actor known for his work in theatre and television. In his first major television role, he portrayed Toby De Silva on the drama series Casualty. He has also appeared on Sherlock and Doctor Who.

Before Fame

He graduated with a bachelor's degree from the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art in 2007.

Trivia

He has acted in a number of William Shakespeare's plays, including Much Ado About Nothing and The Comedy of Errors. He portrayed Iago in a BBC Radio 3 production of Othello. His work in theatre earned him a nomination for the Ian Charleson Award in 2011.

Family Life

He is from the United Kingdom.

Associated With

He shared the screen with Rafe Spall in the 2017 horror film The Ritual.
