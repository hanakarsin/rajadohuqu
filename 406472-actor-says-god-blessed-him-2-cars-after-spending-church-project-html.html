Actor Says God Blessed Him with 2 Cars After Spending on Church Project

Publish date: 2024-07-02

- Actor Williams Uchemba has boldly celebrated the goodness of God in his life

- According to the filmmaker, he spent money in church and received double blessings in return

- Williams mentioned that he had set aside money to buy a car but decided to pump it into the church instead

It is a season of double celebration in the home of popular Nollywood actor, Williams Uchemba as he has just acquired not one, but two SUVs.

The actor, through a post on his Instagram page, narrated the story behind the two latest additions to his garage.

Williams said that in February 2021 he wanted to buy an SUV because he badly needed it.

At the same time, a church project also came up and he was required to contribute.

Torn between his personal desires and doing something for the house of God, the actor finally decided to give part of the money to the church and used the rest to buy a car for someone who needed one.

The actor, with a heart full of gratitude, announced that a month later God decided to bless him with two SUVs.

He mentioned that even though it was a big deal, he was not surprised that God came through for him that way.

"Last month I wanted to buy an SUV (because you need one in Lagos especially if you are always at a Site) and that same period a church building project came up. So I was left with the choice of either buying my SUV or giving money for the church of God to be completed (where people’s life will be changed for good and souls saved). So I was led in my spirit to give it for the church project and used the rest to buy a car for someone who didn’t have one," the star wrote.
