Ali Mandhry Pens Sweet Message to Khalid Al Ameri, Family after Their Kenyan Visit: &quot;Tunawapenda&quot;

Publish date: 2024-03-03
  • Kenya's celebrity chef Ali Mandhry penned a beautiful message to vlogger Khalid Al Ameri and his family
  • The popular UAE vlogger and his family very recently toured Kenya to explore
  • Mandhry told them that their visit will eternally be remembered because they touched many hearts

Celebrated chef Ali Mandhry has taken to social media to appreciate UAE vlogger Khalid Al Ameri and his family for visiting Kenya.

The popular UAE vlogger and his family very recently toured Kenya to explore

Mandhry shared a photo of himself, Khalid Ameri and his family on Instagram.

He thanked them for visiting Kenya and expressed his love to the vlogger and his family.

In the heartwarming letter, the hotelier said that Ameri's visit to Kenya will eternally be cherished since the vlogger and his family made an impact by their visit.

Mandhry stated that Ameri and his family's visit to Kenya touched people's hearts.

"As you touched down the country, you touched our hearts, even more, leaving a beautiful mark that will be cherished forever and ever. Truly amazing souls. We love you all," Mandhry stated.

Mandhry's fans headed to the comment section to appreciate Amery and his family for visiting Kenya.

Khalid Al Ameri, family arrival in Kenya

Announcing his visit, the Vlogger took to his Facebook page where he shared a photo of himself and his family in Mombasa, followed by the caption:

"We have just touched down in Mombasa, Kenya and we can’t wait to explore this beautiful country and meet its incredible people."
