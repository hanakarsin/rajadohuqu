BBNaijas Maria Speaks On Snatching Cubana Chiefpriest Sisters Husband

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Former Big Brother Naija, ‘Shine Ya Eye’ housemate, Maria Chike Benjamin has reacted to the allegation of snatching celebrity barman, Cubana Chief priest’s sister’s husband, Kelvin.

According to reports, a Nigerian Journalist, is said to have contacted the former housemate on Monday 25th October to get her side of the story over the trending issue of snatching a married man and threatening his wife.

It was earlier reported that popular celebrity barman, Cubana Chief priest via his Instagram on Sunday call out the former housemate, Maria accusing her of snatching his sister’s husband.

In an Instagram post on Sunday, Cubana Chief Priest accused Maria of also sending death threats to his sister, claiming that the former housemate is hanging out with his in-law, known as Kelvin.

Sharing a video of himself spraying cash on his sister and in-law while they danced, Cubana Chief Priest said, “@grandfish1 @iam_lolofish_27 I get dollars for you, I get pounds for you, there is nothing sweeter than family. Wives stay woke, girls like @mariachikebemjamin are coming for your husbands. She stole my sister’s husband, as if it was not enough, she is sending her threats. That I won’t let it slide, Kelvin I Dey find una come that Dubai. Nothing must happen to my sister.”

However, when the journalist questioned the reality star on the lingering allegation, she said, it is well while appreciating the journalist who reached out to her.
