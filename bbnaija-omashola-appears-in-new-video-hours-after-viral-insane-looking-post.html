BBNaija Omashola Appears In New Video Hours After Viral 'Insane' Looking Post

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Big Brother Naija reality star, Omashola has appeared in new video clip looking ‘hale and hearty’.

Naija News reports that there have been expression of fears on social media platforms after the reality star was seen on the street looking pale in rag wears.

Former reality star, Ike Onyema who is a friend to Omashola posted the video on his Instagram page on Wednesday(yesterday).

Nigerians have since registered their fears on different social media platforms praying that the video is an extract from a skit or movie and not the new reality of the reality star.

Sharing the scary video earlier, Ike wrote: “I hope this is some sort of skit or movie @sholzy23 After seeing your last post, should I be worried? You haven’t been active on social media since your last post…call me!”

Omashola had in a post on Instagram disclosed that he is facing some pressures and being pushed to limits.

Omashola though did not mention what his real challenges are, he, however, made it clear that he is suffering from depression.

He went on to say that he was lonely in a world surrounded by many, adding that only God can understand his situation.

Omashola wrote: “They say in your youth, depression is mostly peer pressure and the strife to not want to be left behind. In old age, depression is a bucket full of regrets and it is worse than the former.

“Thank God I’m still young but plenty pressure man dey face. I believe in myself normally based on who I be and it is me and my team till the wheels fall off but right now ehn, only God fit understand…. I’m lonely in a world surrounded by people.

“Trying to keep my cool but slowly reaching my breaking point.”

Naija News understands that Omashola’s last Instagram post further heightened the fear that he could be truly unwell.

However, after causing a barrage of reactions on social media, Omashola has appeared in a new video giving his worried fans some vibes.

He was shown in the new video clip with the same rag wears but acting a scene this time.
