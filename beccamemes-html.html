Publish date: 2024-02-10

Conversational storytime and lip-sync content creator who rose to fame through the use of her self-titled account. She has more than 2.8 million followers on the platform. She shares content about her daily life in vlogs on her becca means YouTube channel. 

Before Fame

She began her career as a content creator in 2021. In June of that year, she published a YouTube video titled, "MY VERY FIRST VLOG *EMOTIONAL*."

Trivia

Her "Late night one way chats" TikTok went viral with more than 1 million combined views in total. In November 2021, she did a virtual fashion show and offered discounts to her Instagram followers.

Family Life

She was born in the United States. 

Associated With
