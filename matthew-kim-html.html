Matthew Kim - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-08

Best recognized as one member of the South Korean pop group K.A.R.D. The DSP Media group debuted in December 2016 with the track "Oh NaNa."  He also released a single titled "LOST IN EUPHORIA" in 2022.

Before Fame

Before signing with DSP Media, he auditioned for the first season of K-Pop Star in 2012. 

Trivia

One of his first appearances was in the music video for Rainbow's "Sunshine" released in 2013. He has collaborated with Paris fashion-brand KENZO and designer Nigo in 2022.

Family Life

Though both of his parents are Korean, he grew up in Southern California and even attended California State Polytechnic University. 

Associated With

The other members of K.A.R.D include Jiwoo Jeon, J.Seph, and Somin Jeon.
