BBNaija: '0.89% Votes Over 1.54%'

Publish date: 2024-02-16

Some Nigerians have expressed displeasure over the eviction of Big Brother Naija ‘All-Stars’ Housemate, Princess.

Naija News reports that Princess was evicted from the reality TV show by a jury during the Sunday live show.

Recall that the show host, Ebuka Obi-Uchendu, announced last week that a jury would be introduced in the ‘All-Stars’ edition, and they would determine who will be evicted every Sunday.

Three former BBNaija housemates that were introduced yesterday as members of the jury for the ‘All-Stars’ edition include, Bisola, Dorathy and Mike.

They were sent an envelope with the names of the two housemates who secured the lowest votes, and the jury voted out Princess during the live show.

However, many viewers frowned over the development, considering Princess had secured more votes than Seyi.

On the contrary, many said her eviction was expected because he had been silent in the house for the past two weeks.
