Photo of Notorious Kidnapper 'Lion' In Church, Looking All Emotional Causes Stir Online

Publish date: 2024-06-10

Following the arrest of notorious Bayelsa state kidnapper, John Lion, in Abuja, a photo of him during church service has caused a stir online.

Naija News reports that in the photo making the rounds online, ‘Lion’ was captured wearing a blue native outfit looking all emotional during a worship session in church.

The photo has generated several reactions online as some netizens claimed that crying in church during worship doesn’t make an individual holy.

Other people who do not seem surprised at ‘Lion‘ being in church linked the religious worship centre to a hospital where everyone goes for treatment and not everyone also responds to treatment.

@drpenking wrote: “For those of you who go to church to look for “God Fearing Man”, be aware that the church is just like the hospital.-Not everyone is responding to treatment. Crying during worship doesn’t make someone holy. This is Lyon the Kidnapper who tortures, maims and kills Nigerians.”

@symplyDAPO wrote: “Tell God to thank you in your closet. Most times we unveil ourselves to kidnappers through our testimonies in church. They are everywhere, even in the church. The picture below!!! This is I.K Lyon also known as John Lyon the just arrested kidnapper in Abuja”

@daddyfrz wrote: “A ‘creaminaI’ Lyon the kid*nap*er praying just the same way politicians who destroyed our economy pray in churches and mosques..If Nigerian churches taught their congregations to show love to one another and serve one another in love instead of focusing on prayer, financial growth and destiny helpers Nigeria would be such a great place.”

@charmingnight_ wrote: “Crying during worship doesn’t make them holy, guilt is what makes them cry before the Holy God”

@finest_bone_ever wrote: “Wahala for rich church wey like to give testimony”

@omolay09 wrote: “Why are you guys surprised? Like Olosho no the church choir or ushering department  funny right”

@grim_dude1 wrote: “Praying for successful operations. God don suffer for humans hand criminals”
