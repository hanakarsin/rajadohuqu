Leo Mills - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Social star best known as the younger brother of Max and Harvey Mills. 

Before Fame

He earned a scholarship as a Queens Chorister, affording him to the opportunity to attend boarding school and sing in the chapel choir six days a week.

Trivia

He began composing his own songs in addition to already being skilled as a pianist and trombonist. In September of 2020, he posted a video of himself doing a plank exercise in front of his school mates. 

Family Life

He is the middle child between twins Max and Harvey and younger sister Tilly. His parents are Sara and Paul Mills.

Associated With

He posted a video to musical.ly that featured Richard McCourt and Dominic Wood of the comedy duo Dick and Dom.
