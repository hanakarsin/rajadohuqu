Sophie Conder - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-25

YouTube star who appears on her family's YouTube channel titled Dad V Girls, where her dad competes in various games against his four girls and his wife. The family has amassed over 820 million views to-date. 

Before Fame

The family is from The United Kingdom.

Trivia

The family played a game called "Who knows Sophie best," where her two older sisters competed against each other and their father to see who could guess what she would answer to a series of questions. 

Family Life

She has two older sisters named Kaci and Grace and younger sisters Chloe and Madison. Her father is Joel Conder and her mom is Sarah.

Associated With

She and her family visited Disney World in 2022.
