16 enticing photos from Uganda's artsy Nyege Nyege festival

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Every year, revelers from across the globe get up, dress up and show up to Uganda based Nyege Nyege festival with hopes of a 'lit', fun filled night.

And as usual, the annual festival did not disappoint in 2019 at all especially with its artsy and eccentric theme.

From the photos and videos shared all over social media, it is quite easy to tell that thrill seekers had a time of their lives at the concert.

From throwback songs to old soul music that could just make ones' heart skip a beat, everything about the event made it a night to remember.

As always, Kenyans flocked the festival draped in daring attire that showed off their 'fundamentals' and ravishing bodies.

And when it came to dancing, they danced like no one was watching!

To identify their fellow countrymen, the excited lot would often travel in groups shouting "Wamlambez" with hopes that someone or a group of revelers would respond with a loud "Wamnyonyez".

If something was quite evident, it was the East African spirit that never seems to die.

People had their bodies covered in paint with drawings that brought art to life.

If you missed it then all we can say is we are sorry and better luck next time.
