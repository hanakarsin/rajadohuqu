Breaking News: Another Bridge Blown Up By Boko Haram

Publish date: 2024-06-09

Boko Haram has allegedly blown up another bridge linking Borno and Adamawa States, military sources revealed.

According to Sahara reporters the sources state that the terrorist have continued their offensive in the northeast, attacking residents of Limankara community in Gwoza local government area of Borno State, blazing about 300 houses.

The source revealed that Borno State Commissioner of Trade and Commerce father lost his house to the fire set by the sect.

One unfortunate resident, Titus Musa revealed that the sect took his car and carted away “over three thousands bags of foodstuffs and 10 vehicles.”

The Church of Christ in Nations (COCIN) building in the community was also burned to the ground by the terrorists. “We are helpless,” one of the residents said.

“We don't deserve this. Let the government do something fast about these demons before they finish [off] our generations.”
