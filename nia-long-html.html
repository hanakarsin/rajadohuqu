Nia Long - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-15

Actress who starred on the television shows The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air and Third Watch and has also appeared in such movies as Boiler Room, Big Momma's House, and Are We There Yet?

Before Fame

She had a role in the 1991 iconic film Boyz n the Hood, and that role led to many other opportunities.

Trivia

She appeared in the music video for the Kanye West song "Touch the Sky."

Family Life

She had a son named Massai Zhivago Dorsey II on November 26, 2000. In 2010 she married basketball player Ime Udoka and they welcomed a son named Kez in 2011. 

Associated With

She starred in the movie The Best Man alongside Taye Diggs.
