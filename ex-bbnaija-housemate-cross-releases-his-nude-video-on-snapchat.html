Ex-BBNaija Housemate, Cross Releases His Nude Video On Snapchat

Publish date: 2024-06-14

Former Big Brother Naija housemate, Ikechukwu Sunday Cross Okonkwo, better known as Cross, has drawn reactions on social media after posting his nude video.

Naija News understands that the 30-year-old Anambra born reality star posted the nude video on his Snapchat account today.

Cross has, however, deleted the post which has generated a barrage of reactions on social media platforms.

It was unclear whether Cross intentionally posted the video or it was a mistake, his caption to the already deleted video, however, read, “Shower Time”.

Naija News understands that the reality star’s tattoos and manhood were all glaring in the post now viral on the internet.

Meanwhile, a former Big Brother Naija Shine Ya Eye housemate, Yousef, has announced the demise of his elder brother.

The development was revealed in a post shared by Yousef on his Instagram account on Tuesday.

Yousef in the post noted that he will be away from social media for a while due to the death of his brother.

The former housemate called on his followers to put him and his family in prayers as he mourns his brother.

He wrote, “Hello everyone I just lost my eldest brother and I am going to be offline for a bit.

“Your prayers and condolences will be immensely appreciated. Thank you.”
