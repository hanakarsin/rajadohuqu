Man leaves court bursting in laughter on disclosing why he climbed up State House fence

Publish date: 2024-07-19

- It is not the first time an intruder is scaling up the walls at State House 

A magistrate's court in Nairobi on Wednesday March 9, 2016 was stunned after a 29-year old opened up on his intentions of intruding into State House, Nairobi.

William Njuguna told chief magistrate Daniel Ogembo that he wanted to see how State House looks, and that is why he was trespassing into the compound before General Service Unit (GSU) officers on patrol arrested him.

The man said he was arrested before he could take a good glance at the house on the hill.

It is reported that on March 6, the man left Uhuru Park, passed by Central Park and later went to State House.

Just four months ago, another man, Walter Juma, was arrested by GSU in State House, Nairobi on his way to greet his ‘friend’ Uhuru Kenyatta and ask for assistance as he was living in dejection.
