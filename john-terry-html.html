John Terry - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-07

Longtime centreback for Chelsea FC and England's national team who was named UEFA Club Defender of the Year in 2005, 2008 and 2009. 

Before Fame

He began his youth career at age 11 with West Ham United. 

Trivia

He was named PFA Player of the Year in 2004 and Chelsea Player of the Year in 2001 and 2006. 

Family Life

He has a pair of twins, Georgie and Summer, with his wife Toni Terry, whom he married in 2007. 

Associated With

He was teammates with striker Didier Drogba on Chelsea from 2004 to 2012.
