Milan Espada - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-15

Son of television host Andrea Espada and social media star 4kpapi. He has appeared on the Royalty Family YouTube channel and has gained over 310,000 followers on his personal Instagram account. He has been photographed wearing baby clothes from designers like Burberry and Fendi. 

Before Fame

His parents began posting on his princemilan Instagram account in January 2020. 

Trivia

His parents vlogged his birth for their family YouTube channel. For Halloween in 2020 his parents dressed him up as Woody from Toy Story. 

Family Life

He has an older half-brother named Ferran.

Associated With
