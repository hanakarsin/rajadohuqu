McKaylee True - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-17

Season two champion of Abby's Ultimate Dance Competition who was awarded a scholarship to Joffrey's Young Dancer Program in New York along with a cash prize as a result.

Before Fame

Her mother Shari enrolled her in dance at a young age and is the owner of True Dance Company, where McKaylee began her training.

Trivia

If she does not become a professional dancer, she has hopes of becoming a marine biologist. She has more than 40,000 followers on Instagram.

Family Life

She was born and raised in Lincoln, Nebraska.

Associated With

She grew close with Gianna Martello on Abby's Ultimate Dance Competition.
