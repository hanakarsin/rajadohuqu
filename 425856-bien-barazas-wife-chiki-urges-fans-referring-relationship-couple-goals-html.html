Bien Barazas Wife Chiki Urges Fans Against Referring to Them As Couple Goals: Just a Happy Cou

Publish date: 2024-05-12
  • Chiki Kuruka was quick to note that she and her husband Bien Baraza were not couple goals for their beloved followers
  • After fans went on her comment section to admire them, saying they are their "goals", she quickly refuted the interpretation
  • The dancer noted that she and Bien are just a happy couple with their own journey, just like everyone else

Sauti Sol's Bien Baraza's wife, Chiki Kuruka, believes that every relationship is unique in its own way, and no couple can be compared to another.

The beautiful lass had to caution fans from referring to her relationship with the singer as "couple goals", believing they can create their own happiness that does not follow in their footsteps.

This was after she shared a nice snap of her and Bien as she relished the times spent on their mini-vacay.

She wrote:

"Back to reality, It feels like our little time away was a beautiful dream."

Going on her comment section, many fans responded to her post celebrating the couple, with other fans noting they are the "goals" desired in their relationships.

However, the dancer felt it was a wrong perception of happiness and replied to those fans noting they are not "goals" but a happy couple.

"No, just a happy couple. Who are normal and flawed... We all have our own journeys that work for us," she wrote.

Bien's take on Monogamy

Previously, in an interview on the Singleton Stories with Biko Zulu on NTV, Bien was asked about how he is able to survive in marriage as an artiste.

In the video shared on Facebook, Bien noted he has been married for a year but still has difficulties understanding the concept of monogamy.

The singer said it is still a foreign concept to him in that people expect their partners to give their whole lives to them for the rest of their lives together.

"Don't get me wrong, for some people it works, for some it doesn't. For me as an artiste and for other musicians or just people who have startups. A startup is the same thing as art in the sense that the bearer of the vision is someone who is obsessed on their work. People who have an obsession on their work have a very difficult time making anything else that their work," he said.

He lauded his wife, saying she is so different from anyone he has ever dated and nothing prepared him to be with her.
