Kashway loan app review: download, application, repayment, is it safe?

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Every other day, people lose jobs or come face to face with salary cuts. To be able to survive during these tough economic times, mobile loan applications have become the go-to solution for many. The Kashway loan app is one of the available options.

With only a smartphone, anyone can access the Kashway loan app. It is owned by Wakanda Credit Limited. Given the tough conditions required by financial institutions to be able to acquire funds, more and more Kenyans are turning to this easy-to-access and fast loan.

How do I access Kashway loans?

To borrow from Kashway, you only need a smartphone and an MPesa account. Upon registration, the user is eligible to borrow. The minimum amount a client can borrow is KShs 500, and the maximum is KShs 50,000. Users who apply for this service must be 18 years and above.

How does Kashway loan app work?

The application allows users to apply for a loan by registering their Mpesa number and then apply for the amount they need. After this, the application gets approved. The client then receives the money through MPesa within minutes.

With such a simple application process, many may ask, ''Is KashWay app safe?'' Yes. The app uses the data on the user's phone, including their information, to verify their identity and create a credit score. They then encrypt the data that the user shares to protect their privacy. This information is never shared with third parties.

How to repay a Kashway loan

The shortest repayment period for funds borrowed is 91 days, while the longest is 180 days. The interest rate is 36% APR (Annual Percentage Rate). No service fee is charged. Early payment is encouraged as it helps to increase the loan limit.

KashWay Paybill number is 900068. Your account number is your phone number. Currently, there is no Kashway USSD code. Loan defaulters will have their financial and credit details sent to the Credit Reference Bureau (CRB). Late fees rate will also be applied.
