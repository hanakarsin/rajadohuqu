Noelle Leyva - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-28

TikTok star who is best recognized for the dance and lip sync videos she posts to her account. She often posts fitness content and films videos on the beach with her friends. Her fans have boosted her to over 850,000 followers on the social media platform.

Before Fame

She was a competitive dancer. 

Trivia

Her first TikTok to receive over 1 million views features her dancing to the Wale song "Lotus Flower Bomb."  She gained popularity on Instagram surpassing 550,000 followers via several selfie photos from the gym.

Family Life

She has lived in northern New Jersey. Her grandfather is Cuban. Her grandmother appeared on her TikTok account in December 2020.

Associated With

She posted a TikTok dancing to the Kevin Gates song "Me Too" in December 2020.
