Josh Ramsay - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-09

Canadian songwriter and pop punk lead singer for Marianas Trench. The band's first album, Fix Me, was certified gold while their next two, Masterpiece Theatre and Ever After, were certified platinum. 

Before Fame

At seventeen years old, he appeared as a background vocalist. His first release with Marianas Trench was a self-titled EP, which included songs that would later be released on their first major debut, Fix Me. 

Trivia

The band's earned several Juno Award nominations and in 2013, they won the Juno Award for Group of the Year.

Family Life

He has four sisters: Angela, Sara, Shelly, and Shawn. He and Sara play in a band together called Ramsay Fiction. He married Amanda McEwan in 2020. 

Associated With

He was the songwriter and producer for Carly Rae Jepsen's songs "Call Me Maybe" and "Guitar String/Wedding Ring."
