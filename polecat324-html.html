Polecat324 - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-06

Gamer popular on YouTube for his highly-watched content featuring the game Grand Theft Auto, including roleplay series such as Department of Justice Cops, Code Zero Cops and LSPDFR. He was the director and owner of the Department of Justice Roleplay series. 

Before Fame

He launched his YouTube channel in January of 2013, uploading a first video a month later called "GTA IV LCPDFR MSES Clan Episode 1."

Trivia

He and a number of friends formed a gaming community called Code Zero Gaming. His main YouTube channel has 1.2 million subscribers, and he created a channel to post his live streams called Polecat324 Live, which has earned more than 100,000 subscribers.

Family Life

His first name is Jordan; he was raised in California. He has a Golden Retriever named Delilah who's often featured on his Instagram.

Associated With

One of his friends in the YouTube gaming community is Bay Area Buggs.
