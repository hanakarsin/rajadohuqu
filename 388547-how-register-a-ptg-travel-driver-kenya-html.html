How to register as a PTG Travel driver in Kenya

Publish date: 2024-06-02

PTG Travel is one of the leading solution providers in the Kenyan transport sector. The company offers rides and fast pick-ups to the general public. To ensure that exceptional service is offered to its customers, the firm only hires qualified personnel. For you to become a PTG Travel driver, you must meet their minimum requirements.

How do you register as a PTG Travel driver? Which documents do you require? Here is everything you need to know about PTG registration, portal, and driver application.

PTG Travel meaning

PTG is an acronym for Precise Transport Group. It is a subsidiary of the Pewin Group of Companies. It was started in 2009 as Pewin Cabs. In its initial stages, PTG only had four taxi cars. However, today they have grown to over 1000 cars in Mombasa, Kisumu, and Eldoret.

How to join PTG Travel

To sign up with this top-rated Kenyan transport company, you need to meet the following requirements:

  • Driving License: PTG needs you to have a full driving licence with a PSV badge. It should be valid and up-to-date.
  • Offer excellent service: The company has made its fortunes by offering top-notch services to clients. You should be able to maintain this reputation.
  • Your location: You must be in Nairobi, Eldoret, Kisumu, or Mombasa. These are the areas where PTG operates in.

    • Standard class: Carries a maximum of 4 passengers. It should be made from 2010 onwards.

    If you have all the mentioned requirements, then it’s time to get into the application procedure.

    How to register as a PTG Travel driver

    Follow the steps below to register:

  • Connect to the internet: For faster application, make sure you have speeds of above 1MBPS.
  • Open your browser: For PC users, launch your start menu, search for the program in question, and double-tap to open it. On a smartphone, head to your app tray, scroll until you spot your favourite browser. Tap on its icon.
  • Head to the PTG driver portal: You should use the official address to start your application.
  • Enter your name: Use the titles captured on your national Identity card and not nicknames.
  • Provide your email and phone number: These details must be correct. PTG will use them to notify you about your application status.
  • Key in your date of birth: Make sure it matches the one captured on your ID as you will be required to submit it for verification.
  • Select your gender and location: You can only be in Nairobi, Eldoret, Kisumu, or Mombasa.
  • Tap on the Next button: This is a grey button below the last field.
  • Enter your vehicle details: Specify the model, make, and PSV DL Reference.
  • Confirm if your car is compliant: Check the PSV and inspection compliance radio buttons accordingly.
  • Fill in referral details: If you have learned about PTG through this article, then you can credit us.
  • Upload the needed documents: You need PDF documents or photos of the following:

    You have successfully submitted your application. Should you encounter any technical challenges, do not hesitate to contact the admin PTG Travel driver portal.

    PTG Travel driver app

    At PTG, drivers have a separate application. You will only need it if you are approved after your application. Use the following procedure to download it:

  • Open your browser: Make sure you have an active internet connection. Open your app list, scroll until you see the browser in question.
  • Make sure you have enough data: The PTG driver app has a size of about 20MB. You will need around 30MBs of data to install it.
  • Head to the site: The app is available on the PTG website.
  • Tap on the download link: You will see it at the bottom of the page.
  • Allow installation from unknown sources: Head to your settings, then to security, and enable this option.
  • Install the PTG driver app: Go to your downloads folder, click on its icon, accept all the permissions, and tap Install.
