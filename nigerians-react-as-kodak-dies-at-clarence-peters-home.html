Nigerians React As Kodak Dies At Clarence Peters Home

Publish date: 2024-05-11

Nigerian video vixen Love-Divine (LD) popularly known as Kodak Picture was electrocuted last night in Clarence Peter’s Omole residence and the news came as a saddening shock to many Nigerians who have watched the energetic singer grace their TV screens.

Kodak who was very active on social media hitherto her death was working on a project with popular director Clarence Peter.
