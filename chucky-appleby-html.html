Chucky Appleby - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-16

Famous on YouTube for his Grand Theft Auto gaming videos, the web star known as Chucky has accumulated over 340,000 subscribers.

Before Fame

He uploaded his first YouTube video, "MW3 Glitches: NEW Under Crane Glitch!," on February 21, 2012.

Trivia

Although the ChuckyFTW YouTube channel mostly features GTA videos, it also includes popular uploads focusing on Call of Duty and Minecraft.

Family Life

He has chosen to keep his private life tight-lipped, despite maintaining numerous social media accounts. His brother Tommy runs the popular YouTube channel Top5Central.

Associated With

He and YouTuber Fred Pye both rose to fame for their GTA gaming videos.
