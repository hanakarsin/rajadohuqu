Cardi B Files for Divorce From Offset

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Cardi B and Offset are officially calling it quits after three years of marriage over fresh rumours of cheating on her again… and she wants custody of daughter Kulture.

The rapper filed court docs in Georgia indicating she’s seeking a dissolution of marriage. Cardi is listed as the plaintiff and Offset is listed as the defendant. She filed the paperwork on Tuesday.

There’s also this … according to the court docs, obtained by TMZ, Cardi is seeking primary physical custody as well as legal custody of their 2-year-old daughter, Kulture. She also wants Offset to pay child support — unclear how much though at this point. No word on spousal support either, for now at least.

Naija News recalls that Cardi B and Offset secretly married in September 2017, making the announcement public with an on-stage proposal later that month. In April 2018, Cardi revealed her pregnancy on Saturday Night Live. She gave birth to her daughter Kulture Kiari Cephus in July 2018.

In December 2018, Cardi B claimed that she and Offset were splitting. “I’m gonna always have a lot of love for him because he is my daughter’s father,” Cardi said at the time. Shortly after, Offset interrupted Cardi’s set at Rolling Loud with flowers and pleaded, “TAKE ME BACK, CARDI.” The two reconciled, with Offset joining Cardi on stage as she accepted her 2019 Grammy Award for Best Rap Album.
