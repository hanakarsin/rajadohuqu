M3RKMUS1C - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-23

YouTube phenomenon with more than 4.1 million subscribers who posts Minecraft trolling videos, as well as gameplay videos featuring Call of Duty, Grand Theft Auto, and Garry's Mod. He has also hosted the musick channel on YouTube which has featured him playing various string instruments and giving tips. 

Before Fame

He launched his YouTube channel on April 22, 2009, and posted his first video, "skrub's 1st vid," on July 18, 2009.

Trivia

He created a third channel called Nerd Plays where he acts in the persona of a person named Melvin. He has promoted the popular G FUEL energy drink for gamers on his merkmusic Instagram account. 

Family Life

His first name is Erik and he is from Boston, Massachusetts. He did a Q&A video with his fiance Kayrina AKA Sodapopcandy in February 2018.

Associated With

He creates Minecraft trolling videos, like XboxAddictionz.
