I Don't Know if She Is The Biggest Star In The World

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Award winning Nigerian singer Damini Ogulu popularly known as Burna boy has weighed in on an argument which states that American singer Beyonce is overrated.

During a recent interview with Charlamagne Tha God and Angela Yee on The Breakfast Club, Burna was asked to share his thoughts on the debates trailing Beyonce’s legendary status.

Sharing his opinion on the issue, the singer stated that while he might not be so sure if she is biggest star in the world, he is quite certain that she is a star in her own right.

In the video, Charlamagne stated that someone in his studio thinks Beyonce is overhyped and Drake is far better than her when it comes to music.

The interviewer asked Burna if he shares the same view. Replying, the Afro beat maestro  asked the person if he doesn’t think Drake is also overhyped.

Burna Boy featured on Beyonce’s Ja Ara E off her curated album for the Lion King movie in 2019.

My Management Lied When They Said I Had Mental Health Issues

Former Big Brother Naija (BBNaija) Housemate, Tega Dominic has said that her management was not being truthful when they said that she had mental health challenges due to constant trolling from netizens.

Naija News recalls that after Tega left the BBNaija house, she came under heavy backlash for having an alleged affair with a fellow housemate, Boma while being a married woman.

Both parties however stated that they were simply acting a script for the sake of viewership.

Tega also revealed that her marriage had hit the rocks before she went for the reality show.

During the trolling period, the reality TV star put out a tweet that her mental health was affected.

However, speaking during her talk show ‘The Pandora Show’, Tega stated that it was a lie and that her management posted the tweet without her approval.
