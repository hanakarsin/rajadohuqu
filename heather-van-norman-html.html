Heather Van Norman - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-17

Mother of Odell Beckham Jr. She is a former track and field athlete, having competed on Louisiana State University's team throughout her college career. She became the head track coach for Nicholls State University. 

Before Fame

She began her coaching career by working as an assistant coach for Rice University. 

Trivia

She earned All-American titles every year from 1989 to 1995. LSU won five national championships during her time on the team. She trained Gloria Asumnu, who was an All-American track title holder. 

Family Life

She married Olympian Derek Mills. Odell Beckham Jr., Kordell Beckham, and Jasmyne Mills are her three children. 

Associated With

Stan Kroenke is the owner of her son's team, the Los Angeles Rams.
