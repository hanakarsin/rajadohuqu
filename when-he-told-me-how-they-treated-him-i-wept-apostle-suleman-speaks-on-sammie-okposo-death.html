When He Told Me How They Treated Him I Wept

Publish date: 2024-03-21

Apostle Johnson Suleman of the Omega Fire Ministry has weighed in on the passing of popular gospel artiste, Sammie Okposo.

Naija News recalls Okposo who was in the news in January over allegations of infidelity slumped and died on Friday morning in his home at the age of 51.

Reacting to the death of the singer, Suleman took to his Twitter account to disclose some conversations he had with Okposo.

The clergyman said he wept over the phone when Okposo told him how some ministers treated him after his infidelity scandal broke the internet.

He said some people currently posting pictures of the singer and mourning him, failed to reach out to Sammie during the trying moments.

He wrote, “Now they post his pictures. But never reached out in his trying times. He told me what pastors did to him and we both wept.

“Love has left the church. We have more ‘lawyers’ who are ready to judge than intercessors ready to restore. Rest in power my brother.. You conquered”!

In January 2022, an American lady identified as African Doll, based in Texas had accused Sammie Okposo of impregnating and abandoning her.

Reports claimed that the singer was yet to recover from the scandal which may have affected his health prior to his death on Friday.
