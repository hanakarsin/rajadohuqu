James Aggrey Orengo aka Jimmy and Ahmednasir 'Grand Muller' Abdikadir are two senior counsels who have both appeared in courts for cases involving the government and senior political leaders.

Both have gone to extremes to pull strings and tilt cases in their favour where it all seemed impossible and will be remembered for the roles they played in the 2017 Supreme court presidential petition.

TUKO.co.ke pit the two advocates against each other in a poll that Jimmy beat his counterpart hands down.

Wonderful adjectives like brilliant, articulate, calm and collected were used to describe Orengo's mastery of the corridors of justice, contrary to Ahmednasir who was said to be proud.

Perhaps the one thing that has endeared Jimmy to the masses is his ability to see the beyond what other lawyers see, something Ahmednsir has been unable to.

One of Orengo's most known phrase was that the government eats its own people, sentiments he made in the Senate immediately after Jubilee won the elections.

His words were rubbished by many at that time, but less than three years since he made them Jubilee party diehards like deputy president William Ruto, MP Moses Kuria and senator Kipchumba Murkomen have fallen out of favour with the same leadership they once hailed.

The other thing that makes Orengo the people's choice is the fact that he remains among the few lawyers the world over to have successfully represented a presidential candidate and earned a nullification of the results.

That is not to say that Grand Muller does not have those who would choose him over Orengo.

Orengo, who is often described as a walking constitution won the battle of wits on the legal front, making him the uncontested first-choice lawyer for many Kenyans.
