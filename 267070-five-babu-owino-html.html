Five things to know about Babu Owino

Publish date: 2024-04-08

Babu Owino, the Member of Parliament for Embakasi East, is an exceptional political figure. Most of you know Babu Owino as the longest serving student leader to ever be in power at the University of Nairobi. Despite the politician’s clandestine fashion and eloquence, his life is not short of challenges. According to the legislator, he has the objective of transforming the society by using politics as his tool. There are several things about Babu Owino that are unknown to many, and this article will shed light on such facts.

1. Babu Owino real name

The first fact is that ‘Babu’ is just a fictional name and not a real name for the legislator. The Embakasi East Member of Parliament official name is Paul Owino Ongili.

2. Babu Owino humble beginning

Paul Ongili is a successful political figure and businessman with several investments under his name. Despite his current lifestyle, it is worth noting that Babu Owino comes from a humble background. Mr Ongili's mother had to odd jobs including selling fish among others to fend for Babu Owino and his siblings. It is presumed that such hardships molded Babu into the person he is today as he has evolved into a vibrant, determined, and a bold political figure.

3. Babu Owino Biography

Babu Owino was born in Nyalenda Slums in Kisumu. Babu has always been an intelligent student ever since as he scored 394 marks out of 500 earning a place at the Mang'u High school which is one of the best National schools in the country. However, due to lack of money, his family could not afford to take him to that school, and instead, a Good Samaritan offered to pay for the politician's fees at Kisumu Boys High School.

Babu Owino did exceptionally well scoring an A- of 80 points in the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) in 2005. Since Babu passed well, the Joints Admission Board offered him a place at Moi University which he declined. Paul spoke to his uncle who at the time was a prominent farmer, and he introduced him to Agribusiness. Babu Owino started his business of trading vegetable on a large scale, and within two years he had enough money to take himself to a university of his choice. In 2008, he enrolled at the prestigious University of Nairobi to pursue a degree in Actuarial Science.

4. Babu Owino graduated from University

The third fact many may not know about Paul Ongili is that he actually graduated. His academic credentials have been the discussion of many because the MP served as a student leader for six years earning himself the title ‘Robert Mugabe of UoN.’ As a result, in 2017, Babu removed the doubts of many after he decided to go public with his Actuarial Science degree. According to the picture posted on his social media, the legislator graduated from the University of Nairobi with a First Class Honors in August 2012.

5. Babu Owino Speeches

The flamboyant legislator is famous for his speeches which leave his followers wanting more. As intelligent as he is, he is fond of using big English words. Moreover, the Member of Parliament is known for creating several buzzwords such as Tibim, Akchuu, and Tiiaaalaala whose meanings are still mysterious to many.

Apart from the above five this, it is also important to know these other things about Babu:

Late Gaddafi was Babu Owino’s financial sponsor

According to an Interview that Babu Owino did on the Big Question at Citizen Kenya TV, the late Muammar Gadaffi, the former president of Libya was his financial sponsor. Moreover, during the interview, Babu expressed that he had investments in various firms including a supplies company, real estate, and construction company. Mr Owino also still pursues farming. During the interview, he articulated how he was financially orphaned when Gadaffi was killed as he was his best friend and financier. When the interviewer asked about his other financiers, the legislator declined to name them for security reasons.

Babu Owino Cars

Babu Owino leads a very wealthy lifestyle, and these include cruising in high-end automobiles. The politician’s first car was a Toyota Corolla 110. Besides this, Babu Owino owns a Range Rover Sport, Land Cruiser V8, Hyundai Tuscany Sports, and a BMW.

Babu Owino career

Mr Ongili’s political career started when he decided to vie for university politics at the University of Nairobi. During his academic year 2011/2012, Owino was voted as the chairman of the Student Organization of Nairobi University (SONU). Up to date, he is the longest serving student leader in the student union in the country. In 2013, Babu decided to be on the ballot vying as the Member of Parliament for Westlands; however, his opponent Hon Timothy Wanyonyi won the race. Babu did not give up and decided to try one more time in 2017 for Embakasi East Parliamentary post which he emerged victorious.

After several court hearings, Justice Joseph Sergon ordered on Monday, February 12 for a vote recount in the affected constituency following an evidence of several discrepancies on results forms.
