Alex Fine - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-30

Athlete who is famous for his Alex Fine Performance brand of personal training. He has provided personal nutritional and fitness regimens for celebrities and professional athletes, including Jennifer Aniston and Odell Beckham Jr. 

Before Fame

He earned his bachelor's degree from Central Michigan University in 2015. He competed as part of their football team. 

Trivia

He has seen his alexfine44 Instagram account grow to boast more than 360,000 followers for his everyday fitness tips. He has featured his in-depth workouts with Eric Kendricks.

Family Life

He is married to singer Cassie Ventura. They welcomed a daughter named Frankie in December 2019, and in March 2021 they welcomed their second daughter named Sunny.

Associated With

He and Charles Melton have worked together as clients.
