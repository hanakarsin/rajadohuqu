4 Men With The Most Wives

Publish date: 2024-02-01

For some men one wife is absolutely enough. But for some men there are no limits.

Meet the four men who can't stop 'marrying'. Two of them are Nigerians, so no wonder the population of our country is getting closer and closer to 180 million people.

1. Saleh Al-Sayeri (Saudi Arabia)

This 64-year-old businessman has had 58 wives, but he doesn't remember the names of most of them. The Islamic law allows men to have up to four wives at a time, but he says he has kept the same three for the past 20 years or more. It's the fourth wife he rotates.  "I like to change my fourth wife every year," Saleh boasted.

2. Ziona Chana (India)

This man thinks that he has the biggest family in the world. He has 94 children, 14-daughters-in-law and 33 grandchildren. And 39 wives as well. All of them live in India in a 100-rooms house.

"I feel like God's special child. He's given me so many people to look after.

I consider myself a lucky man to be the husband of 39 women and head of the world's largest family," he stated to reporters.

3. Bello Maasaba (Nigeria)

Bello Maasaba is a Nigerian Muslim. He claims he has 107 wives. He currently lives with 86 of them. Bello used to have only 2 wives, but later he decided he needs more.

"I get a revelation from God telling me any woman I'm going to marry. If it wasn't from God, I wouldn't have gone beyond two," he explains in a wispy voice. In his 90 years he still being a good husband who is caring, loving, and capable of giving his wives all they need.

4. Simon Ado (Nigeria)

Simon, also known as Onuwa, African Solomon or Satan is Sleeping, is the second Nigerian in our list. He is a 68-years-old herbalist from Enugu state. Currently he has 58 wives and nearly 300 children. And he is not going to stop. The man even asked Igboeze North Local Government to build his own village for his family. He intends to name the new Onuwa.
