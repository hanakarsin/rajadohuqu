Premier League Matchweek Three Fixtures, Kick-off Time

Publish date: 0001-01-01

The 2023-2024 Premier League matchweek three will kick off on Friday, August 25, when newly promoted Luton Town visit Stamford Bridge to face Chelsea at 8 p.m.

Coach Mauricio Pochettino and his Blues boys are yet to win any match so far this season and have recorded one draw against Liverpool, and a defeat against West Ham United, already this season. Hence, the game against Luton is a must-win for Chelsea as they attempt to contend for the Premier League title this season.

On Saturday, August 26, there will be six Premier League games scattered across England and the clashes to watch out for are Bournemouth vs. Tottenham, Arsenal vs. Fulham, and Manchester United vs. Nottingham Forest.

On Sunday, August 27, there will be three Premier League matchweek three games. The biggest clash of the week will take place at St. James Park between the new big boys on the block, Newcastle United and Liverpool.

Below are the complete 2023-2024 Premier League match week three fixtures:
