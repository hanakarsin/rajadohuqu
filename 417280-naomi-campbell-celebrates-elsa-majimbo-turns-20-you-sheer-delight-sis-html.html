Elsa Majimbo relocates to South Africa

In other entertainment news, TUKO.co.ke reported the star relocated to South Africa because she felt Kenya was not safe anymore for her.

The online influencer said she felt freer in South Africa than she did in Nairobi and explained that she had had a bad relationship with Kenya, and so the moment she left the country, she never looked back.

She also thanked South Africans for supporting her when Kenyans did not find her content amusing.
