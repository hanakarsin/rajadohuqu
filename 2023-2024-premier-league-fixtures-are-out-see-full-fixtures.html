2023-2024 Premier League Fixtures Are Out: See Full Fixtures

Publish date: 2024-06-21

The 2023-2024 Premier League fixtures were officially released today, June 15, 2023. Based on the released fixtures, the 2023-2024 Premier League season will commence on the weekend of August 11-13 and end on May 19 2024.

This means that the 2023-2024 Premier League fixtures will be exhausted after nine months.

In between the nine months, there will be a mid-season player break from January 14-20.

The 2023-2023 Premier League campaign will commence with the Community Shield between Manchester City (winners of the Premier League) and Arsenal (second runner-up of the league) since City are the winners of the FA Cup. The match will take place on August 6, 2023.
