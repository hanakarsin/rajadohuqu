Jacksepticeye - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-07

Energetic video game commentator who features content on games such as Five Nights at Freddy's, Grand Theft Auto and God of War on his jacksepticeye YouTube channel. The channel has earned over 30 million subscribers. He has also diversified his content, posting videos where he recaps life events, comments on TikToks and reacts to scary videos. 

Before Fame

He started making YouTube videos full time after graduating with a hotel management degree.

Trivia

One of his most popular videos is called "Mirror's Edge with the Oculus Rift." He signed on to create exclusive content through Twitch in a deal with Disney Digital Network in 2018. 

Family Life

Born Sean McLoughlin, he grew up with two brothers and two sisters in Ireland. His father passed away in January 2021. He dated Signe Hansen up until mid 2018. In 2019, he began dating Dutch YouTuber Girl Gamer Gab.

Associated With

He has collaborated on videos with other popular YouTubers such as PewDiePie and Markiplier.
