Camren Bicondova - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Versatile talent who was part of the all-girl dance group 8 Flavahz who finished in second place on season 7 of America's Best Dance Crew and who landed the role of Selina Kyle on the Fox Batman television series Gotham. 

Before Fame

She was discovered by record producer and film director Chris Stokes who put her in his musical-drama Battlefield America.

Trivia

She was an Elite Protege with The Pulse on Tour and was a part of the Coke RED Moves campaign commercial. She is also known by the nickname Lil Mis Cam Cam.

Family Life

She was born in San Diego, California and her family later relocated to Hawaii.

Associated With

She was featured in the music video for the Ciara song "Got Me Good."
