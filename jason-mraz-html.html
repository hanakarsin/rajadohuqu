Jason Mraz - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-24

Acoustic guitarist and singer whose 2002 single "The Remedy (I Won't Worry)" propelled him to fame. He released his third album, We Sing. We Dance. We Steal Things., in 2008, and the album's first single, "I'm Yours," spent 76 weeks on the U.S. Hot 100 chart. 

Before Fame

His parents, Tom and June Mraz, divorced when Jason was four, and he attended Lee-Davis High School and AMDA College in New York.

Trivia

His last name means "frost" in Czech.

Family Life

He married makeup artist Sheridan Edley Mraz in 2001, yet they divorced later that year. He was briefly engaged to fellow singer/songwriter Tristan Prettyman. He then married Christina Carano in 2015. They divorced in 2023.

Associated With

He performed as the opening act for an Alanis Morissette concert in 2005.
