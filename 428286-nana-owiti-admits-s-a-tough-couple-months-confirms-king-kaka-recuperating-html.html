Nana Owiti Admits It's Been a Tough Couple of Months, Confirms King Kaka Is Recuperating

Publish date: 2024-05-24
  • Nana Owiti has assured fans that King Kaka is doing great after he was discharged from the hospital
  • The Wajinga Nyinyi hitmaker has been going for checkups on a daily basis, and he is yet to reveal what's ailing him
  • On September 1, 2021, he narrated a touching story, revealing that he had lost 33kgs after he was misdiagnosed
  • The father of three penned a sweet message to his wife of 10 years, Nana Owiti, praising her for being by his side during the three months he was bedridden

Nana Owiti, wife of celebrated rapper King Kaka, has revealed that he is recuperating at home after spending three months in hospital.

During a Q&A with her followers on Instagram, Nana responded to several questions about King Kaka's health status, assuring fans that he is doing well.

"It has been a tough couple of months but right now God has been amazing. We are much better," she said.

"What illness is King Kaka suffering from?" a fan asked.

Responding to them, the mom of two said:

"He will share with y'all when he is ready."

She also revealed that he had added 6kgs.

Nana, while responding to another fan who praised her for being such a great and supportive partner, he said:

"We (Kaka and I) are blessed to have each other."

Another fan how she knew that Kaka was the right man for her, and she said:

"I honestly didn't know. Time and consistency exposed who he was and what I was looking for aligned with what he continuously offered."

Kaka praises Nana

The father of three penned a sweet message to his wife of 10 years, Nana Owiti, praising her for being by his side during the three months he was bedridden.

"The last three months has been nothing short but terrifying. This lady here @nanaowiti has shown and proven what a wife and a best friend is. From kulala kwa couch ya hospitali next to my bed then you wake up shower in the same hospital, go and smile kwa TV like everything is okay to fighting with nurses on why they are slow, to praying and encouraging me to eat, to sponge-bathing me when the temperatures were too high," he wrote in part.

The Wajinga Nyinyi hitmaker continued:

"I am currently walking on that road to recovery but I pray that God guides you and grants you all your hearts’ wishes and desires. Just learnt the new meaning of the word 'partner' in a relationship. Be blessed and Asante Nana."
