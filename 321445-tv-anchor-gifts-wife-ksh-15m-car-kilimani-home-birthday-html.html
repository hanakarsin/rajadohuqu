TV anchor gifts wife with KSh 1.5M car, new Kilimani home for birthday

Publish date: 2024-02-18

KBC news anchor Bonnie Musambi has once again shown his romantic prowess after showering his wife with yet another car and a newly furnished house for her birthday.

The anchor surprised his wife, Betty Musambi, with a brand new Nissan X Trail worth KSh 1.5 million and a brand new house in the posh Kilimani estate.

Speaking to TUKO.co.ke, the ecstatic wife said her loving husband gifted her the expensive goodies for the amazing journey they have had together in life.

“We were heading to church on Sunday, October 27, and he told me he was going downstairs to pick a carpet just before we leave. A few minutes later, he called me down and I unsuspiciously strode downstairs only to find him dangling car keys, I was so happy," Betty said.

Two days earlier, on Friday, October 25, she was heading out to pick her children from school when her secretary offered to accompany her to the school.

The employee had apparently conspired with Musambi to surprise Betty with the furnished home.

The secretary asked her to take a detour to the new house before going to pick up the children and to her pleasant surprise, was it was fully furnished.

Last year, Musambi had surprised his wife with a Toyota Passo for her birthday.

He orchestrated the plan with Betty's friends and family.

Both Musambi and Betty are media personalities and have been an item since 2012.

They are blessed with two adorable daughters, Lovelyn Mutanu and Takisha Matanah.

Born in Kitui county in 1981, Musambi has grown over the years in the media circles to become arguably one of the most listened to radio presenter and TV news anchor.
