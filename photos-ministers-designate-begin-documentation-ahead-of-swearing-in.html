Ministers-designate Begin Documentation Ahead Of Swearing In

Publish date: 2024-04-14

Ministers-designate for the administration of President Bola Tinubu on Saturday commenced their documentation exercise ahead of the swearing-in scheduled for Monday, 21st August 2023.

Naija News reports the documentation took place at the Office Of The Secretary To The Government Of The Federation, Abuja.

At the time of filing this report, the exercise was still ongoing.

All the 45 Ministers-designate are expected to complete their documentation by Sunday, 20th August.

President Tinubu Fixes Date To Hold First FEC Meeting

Meanwhile, President Tinubu has reportedly scheduled Wednesday, next week, for his first Federal Executive Council (FEC) meeting since he was sworn in as president on May 29.

Naija News understands that the inaugural FEC will hold after the swearing-in of new ministers into his cabinet on Monday.

It was gathered that the Secretary to the Government of the Federation (SGF), George Akume and the cabinet office are working on the focus of the first FEC which will be more like induction, where President Tinubu is expected to share his priorities under Renewed Hope Agenda with the new cabinet.
