Jared Mecham - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-09

Popular YouTuber who vlogs about his family with his wife Ellie on their channel Ellie and Jared, which has amassed over 1.6 million subscribers. He has also co-run the Ellie + Jared podcast which has been available for listening on Apple Podcasts. 

Before Fame

The Ellie and Jared channel was initially started by Ellie under the name stylebyellie in October of 2011. 

Trivia

He and Ellie struggled with infertility but were able to finally conceive which they revealed by means of a sonogram image in a September 26, 2013 YouTube video. Their "EMOTIONAL LIVE BIRTH!! (Birth Vlog)" video has received over 14 million views since May of 2015. 

Family Life

He and Ellie have sons named Jackson, Calvin and Tommy. He has a brother named Matt. In March 2022 the couple announced that they were expecting a fourth child together. They welcomed a daughter in January 2023.

Associated With

He is the patriarch of a family vlogging channel like Andres Ehbee.
