All these goods are products that were created and sold as part of a manufacturing business. The chair you are currently sitting on or the cup you are using to drink your coffee, were most likely produced by one of the manufacturing companies.

The manufacturing process is the production of merchandise for use or sale with the help of human labor and automatic machines, different tools, chemical and biological processing, or formulation, according to Wikipedia. Frankly speaking, manufacturing process is when raw materials are transformed into finished goods on a large scale for further sale.

Bamburi Special Products Ltd – BSP

If you have heard about the famous Kenyan Bamburi Cement Limited, you might already know that Bamburi Special Products Ltd – BSP is their wholly owned subsidiary. In its turn, Bamburi Cement Limited is a subsidiary of Lafarge - the biggest building material manufacturer in the whole world. Moreover, Bamburi Special Products Ltd. is a proud member of the Concrete Manufacturers Association (CMA) of South Africa.

The company was founded in 1998 and has fully changed the Concrete Paving Industry in South Africa. They call themselves "the pioneer in the development of concrete paving solutions" and during the period of 20 years have become the largest supplier of Concrete blocks in the country.

Athi River Mining Ltd

Athi River Mining Limited, or ARM Cement Limited how it is called today, is one of the biggest and well-known mining and manufacturing companies in Kenya, as well as the biggest economy in the East African Community. The main office with the headquarters are based in Nairobi office and its stock is listed on the Nairobi Stock Exchange.

The main goals of the ARM Cement Limited is to deliver quality in each product according to all international standards, as well as the best customer service. The company is successfully achieving its goals, thanks to deep, unwavering commitment to communication, transparency, ethical behavior and teamwork.

For a better teamwork and mutual understanding among company employees, ARM is constantly organizing job training activities, coaching, mentoring and community service programs.
